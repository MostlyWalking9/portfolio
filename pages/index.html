About

Sebastian Schistek

Creative Game Artist and Graphic Designer working across 2D/3D art, brand identity, and web development. Currently Creative Web Designer & Frontend Developer at Dr.Bohl (Bohl Entertainment) in Vienna, leading a studio's digital platform from concept through launch. Trained in Game Art & 3D Animation (BA, SAE Institute, Vienna).
